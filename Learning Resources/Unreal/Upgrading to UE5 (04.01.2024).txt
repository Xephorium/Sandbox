  Reminder
    Unreal's equivalent of "Invalidate Caches and Restart" is to delete (or backup) the project's Binaries, Build, Intermediate, and Saved folders, then right click Sandbox.uproject in Explorer and "Generate Visual Studio Project Files."

  Problem 3
    Issue
      After enabling Lumen, scene lighting is still WAY too dark with broken shadows and blotchy lighting/reflections.
    Solution
      1. Set all lights to Movable (Lumen doesn't use static lights) to fix shadows
      2. Turn up graphics settings for Global Illumination & Reflections to address blotchy lighting/rreflections
      3. Play with PostProcessingVolume settings to get desired look
         - Exposure > Min/Max Brightness
      4. Tweak light settings to eliminate corner bleed
         - Problem Light > Light > Advanced > Shadow Bias/Shadow Slope Bias
